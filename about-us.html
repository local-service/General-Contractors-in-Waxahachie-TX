About Thompson & Boys LLC

Thompson & Boys LLC stands for commitment, trust, and quality craftsmanship. As a leading general contractor in Waxahachie, TX, they’ve earned a reputation built on reliable service, high standards, and community trust. They treat every project like it’s personal, combining dedication with a skilled team to deliver results that last.

Our Story

Our journey started with the goal of offering dependable services to our community. Beginning as a small operation, we grew by focusing on quality, dedication, and building strong client relationships. With each project, we’ve expanded, going from local home repairs to larger-scale renovations. Our growth didn’t change our core values; At Thompson & Boys LLC,we treat each job as an opportunity to showcase our passion and skill.

Our reputation grew as we took on projects across Waxahachie, TX. We remained committed to our roots, ensuring that every client receives our best. Through the years, our story has been one of gradual, steady growth that’s focused on providing top-notch service with integrity.
